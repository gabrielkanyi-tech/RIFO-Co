About Us

The RIFO Community (RIFO-CO) is a dynamic, youth-led, community-based organization that exists to restore integrity, inspire action, build resilience, and unlock opportunities within grassroots communities across Kenya.

Founded on the values of ethical leadership, empowerment, and innovation, RIFO-CO is committed to driving meaningful transformation from the bottom up.

Vision

To see a society where integrity and opportunity form the foundation of empowered, thriving communities.

Mission

To uplift lives by restoring ethical leadership, inspiring collective action, strengthening community resilience, and creating sustainable pathways for opportunity and growth.

Thematic Areas of Focus

  • Leadership & Governance: Civic education for youth & women, grassroots leadership training, and public dialogue forums.
  • Health & Mental Wellness: Menstrual dignity campaigns, addiction awareness, and access to health information.
  • Education & Legal Literacy: School mentorship, literacy campaigns, and legal clinics.
  • Climate Action & Sustainability: Tree planting, environmental education, and green entrepreneurship.
  • Digital Skills & Innovation: Online income training, coding workshops, and digital mentorship.
